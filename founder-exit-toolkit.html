Dynasty Trust — the key planning lever at this exit scale: A gift of 15% ($15M at exit) into a Dynasty Trust today at the Phase 1 valuation ($20M implied) uses only ~$3M of your $15M single lifetime exemption. All future appreciation — from $3M today to $15M at exit — accrues outside your taxable estate. At 6.5% growth over 10 years, the trust compounds to ~$28M for your children and their descendants, entirely estate-tax free at each generational transfer.
C-Corp conversion opportunity: Gator Creek is currently an LLC — which means §1202 QSBS does not apply. A conversion to C-Corp before exit (with sufficient time to satisfy the 5-year holding period) could add up to $10M+ of tax-free exclusions. Gator Creek was incorporated in 2025 — a 2026 conversion would start the QSBS clock, with the exclusion potentially available from 2031 onwards. Worth a conversation with your CPA and corporate counsel before this window closes.
